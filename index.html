What is Edge AI? 什麼是邊緣AI？

Cloud AI (e.g. ChatGPT) — runs on remote servers; needs internet.
Edge AI — runs directly on your device; works offline, fully private.
Local AI (e.g. Ollama) — runs on your PC but requires installation.

邊緣AI在你的設備上直接運行，無需上傳數據，保護隱私。
